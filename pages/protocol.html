COMPETITION_MATRIX (MCS)

The Multidimensional Contribution Score (MCS) rewards active participation across all club activities.

  • Elite Class Win: 1500 XP - Major Hackathons or Tech-Sprints.
  • Campaign Lead: 800 XP - Organizing campus-wide awareness programs.
  • Workshop Mastery: 300 XP - Active skill acquisition in club sessions.

CLUB_GOVERNANCE

Our clubs operate under a standardized protocol to ensure fair play and equal opportunity for every candidate.

  • Inclusivity: Zero-tolerance policy for harassment in any campaign or event.
  • Meritocracy: Leadership roles are assigned based on total campaign impact.
  • Verification: All participation points must be verified by a Club Lead.

EVENT_STANDARDS

Attendance and hosting are tracked via the System Protocol to maintain transparency.

  • Session Presence: 250 XP for every verified attendance.
  • Volunteer Work: 500 XP for logistics and management roles.
  • Digital Footprint: Social media shares for campaigns grant a 50 XP boost.

CAMPAIGN_TIERS

Earn unique badges based on the type of campus campaigns you support or initiate.

  • Titan: Lead 5+ successful events and reach 20,000 XP.
  • Striker: Support 3+ campaigns and reach 10,000 XP.
  • Scout: Initial entry tier for all registered candidates.

INNOVATION_LABS

Innovation Labs are specialized research cells within clubs dedicated to experimental projects and rapid prototyping.

  • Research Grant: Access to a 2000 XP pool for approved project proposals.
  • Lab Access: Top-tier contributors gain priority access to campus hardware and software licenses.
  • IP Protocol: Projects developed within labs are credited to the student while staying under the club's portfolio.

AMBASSADOR_PROTOCOLS

Ambassadors are the face of Pixel Phantoms, bridging the gap between local clubs and global industry partners.

  • Network Expansion: Earn 1000 XP for facilitating successful partnerships with other campus organizations.
  • Public Relations: Manage the official club handles and campaign communications for a recurring monthly XP stipend.
  • Elite Status: Ambassadors represent the club at national hackathons and inter-college summits.

OFFICIAL_DECLARATION

This protocol governs all Pixel Phantoms chapters. Transparency is our core value. All leaderboard logic is visible and open to audit by the community to ensure total fairness in rankings and rewards.
